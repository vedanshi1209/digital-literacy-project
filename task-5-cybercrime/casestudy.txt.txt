Phishing is a common cybercrime where attackers try to steal personal information such as passwords, bank details, or OTPs. They usually send fake emails or messages that look like they are from trusted sources like banks or websites. When users click on these links, they are redirected to fake websites where their data is stolen.

Students are often targeted because they may not be fully aware of such scams. The consequences can include loss of money, identity theft, and misuse of personal data. It is important to stay alert and avoid clicking on unknown links or sharing sensitive information online.
